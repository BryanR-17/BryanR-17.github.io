Featured Project

EEG Cognitive Engagement Analysis

Studied how different audio conditions affect cognitive engagement by building a full signal-processing and analysis pipeline over multi-subject EEG data.

  • Validated multi-subject EEG data across trials, conditions, sampling rates, and overall structure to confirm dataset integrity before analysis
  • Mapped the experimental design and determined that conditions were interleaved, informing how segments were extracted for comparison
  • Extracted frequency-domain features including alpha and beta band power to quantify cognitive states across conditions
  • Analyzed alpha and beta band effects independently to surface effects that the combined engagement ratio was obscuring
  • Designed and structured the full analysis workflow, refining signal processing steps and implementation across stages
  • Concluded with weak condition-level effects and high cross-subject variability — pointing to individual neural differences as the dominant factor
